EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (06/01/2020)

In Middlesex county, for the week ending on 05/30/2020,

  • there were 94 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 6.9%)
  • there were 16 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (an increase of 33.3%)
  • there were 178 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 14%)
