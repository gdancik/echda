EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (06/13/2020)

In Hartford county, for the week ending on 06/12/2020,

  • there were 442 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 18.1%)
  • there were 42 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (no change from last week)
  • there were 476 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 26%)
