EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (06/29/2020)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 06/27/2020,

  • there were 548 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 17.8%)
  • there were 56 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 5.1%)
  • there were 877.6 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 29.1%)
