EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (06/22/2020)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 06/20/2020,

  • there were 667 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 40.4%)
  • there were 59 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 54.6%)
  • there were 1264.2 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 30.5%)
