EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (06/01/2020)

In Hartford county, for the week ending on 05/30/2020,

  • there were 610 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 36.7%)
  • there were 75 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 29.2%)
  • there were 1027 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 27.9%)
