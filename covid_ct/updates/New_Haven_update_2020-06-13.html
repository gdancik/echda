EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (06/13/2020)

In New Haven county, for the week ending on 06/12/2020,

  • there were 204 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 59.8%)
  • there were 34 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 17.1%)
  • there were 608 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 39.5%)
