EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (06/15/2020)

In Hartford county, for the week ending on 06/13/2020,

  • there were 409 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 20.3%)
  • there were 40 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (an increase of 5.3%)
  • there were 441 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 29.2%)
