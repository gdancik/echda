EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (05/29/2020)

In Fairfield county, for the week ending on 05/27/2020,

  • there were 643 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 29.7%)
  • there were 66 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 24.1%)
  • there were 1574 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 23.5%)
