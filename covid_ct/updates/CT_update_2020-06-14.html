EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (06/15/2020)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 06/13/2020,

  • there were 1120 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 36.6%)
  • there were 130 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (an increase of 2.4%)
  • there were 1855.2 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 31.3%)
