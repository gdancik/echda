EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (06/22/2020)

In Fairfield county, for the week ending on 06/20/2020,

  • there were 169 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 32.4%)
  • there were 15 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 55.9%)
  • there were 444 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 30.2%)
