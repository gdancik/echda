EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (06/08/2020)

In Hartford county, for the week ending on 06/06/2020,

  • there were 513 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 15.9%)
  • there were 38 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 49.3%)
  • there were 623 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 39.3%)
