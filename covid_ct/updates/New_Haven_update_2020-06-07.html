EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (06/08/2020)

In New Haven county, for the week ending on 06/06/2020,

  • there were 505 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (an increase of 20.8%)
  • there were 38 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 44.9%)
  • there were 945 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 39.2%)
