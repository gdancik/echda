EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (08/03/2020)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 07/30/2020,

  • there were 1587 new daily reported confirmed/probable cases (an increase of 122.6%)
  • there were 26 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 25.7%)
  • there were 69 current hospitalizations (an increase of 9.5%)
