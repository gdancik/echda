EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (06/14/2020)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 06/12/2020,

  • there were 1176 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 34.5%)
  • there were 131 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 8.4%)
  • there were 1977.4 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 31.1%)
