EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (06/01/2020)

In Fairfield county, for the week ending on 05/30/2020,

  • there were 476 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 45%)
  • there were 62 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 10.1%)
  • there were 1416 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 20.1%)
