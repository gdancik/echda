EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (05/29/2020)

In New Haven county, for the week ending on 05/27/2020,

  • there were 535 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 43.7%)
  • there were 72 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 32.7%)
  • there were 1772 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 24.3%)
