EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (06/22/2020)

In Hartford county, for the week ending on 06/20/2020,

  • there were 187 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 54.3%)
  • there were 23 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 42.5%)
  • there were 293 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 33.6%)
