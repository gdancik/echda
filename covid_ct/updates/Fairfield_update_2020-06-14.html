EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (06/15/2020)

In Fairfield county, for the week ending on 06/13/2020,

  • there were 250 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 50.7%)
  • there were 34 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 5.6%)
