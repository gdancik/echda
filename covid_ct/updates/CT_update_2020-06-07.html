EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (06/08/2020)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 06/06/2020,

  • there were 1767 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (an increase of 2%)
  • there were 127 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 49.4%)
  • there were 2750.8 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 36%)
