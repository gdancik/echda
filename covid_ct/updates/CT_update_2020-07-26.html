EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (07/27/2020)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 07/23/2020,

  • there were 1140 new daily reported confirmed/probable cases (an increase of 72.7%)
  • there were 33 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 21.4%)
  • there were 71 current hospitalizations (an increase of 6%)
