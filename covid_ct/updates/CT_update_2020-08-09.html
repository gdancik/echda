EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (08/10/2020)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 08/06/2020,

  • there were 780 new daily reported confirmed/probable cases (a decrease of 47.5%)
  • there were 16 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 15.8%)
  • there were 65 current hospitalizations (an increase of 22.6%)
