EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (07/20/2020)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 07/16/2020,

  • there were 785 new daily reported confirmed/probable cases (an increase of 5.2%)
  • there were 53 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (an increase of 130.4%)
  • there were 66 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 25%)
