EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (06/29/2020)

In Fairfield county, for the week ending on 06/27/2020,

  • there were 158 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 6.5%)
  • there were 14 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 6.7%)
  • there were 303 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 31.8%)
