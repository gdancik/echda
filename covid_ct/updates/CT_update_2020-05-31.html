EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (06/01/2020)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 05/30/2020,

  • there were 1733 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 43.2%)
  • there were 251 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 11.9%)
  • there were 4323 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 24.2%)
