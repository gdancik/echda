EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (06/08/2020)

In Middlesex county, for the week ending on 06/06/2020,

  • there were 82 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 12.8%)
  • there were 10 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 37.5%)
  • there were 119 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 33.1%)
