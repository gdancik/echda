EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (05/29/2020)

In New London county, for the week ending on 05/27/2020,

  • there were 189 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (an increase of 130.5%)
  • there were 11 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (no change from last week)
