EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (05/29/2020)

In Middlesex county, for the week ending on 05/27/2020,

  • there were 110 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (an increase of 0.9%)
  • there were 14 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (an increase of 7.7%)
  • there were 190 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 14%)
