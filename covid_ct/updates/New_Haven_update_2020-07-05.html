EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (07/06/2020)

In New Haven county, for the week ending on 07/02/2020,

  • there were 155 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (an increase of 31.4%)
  • there were 10 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 23.1%)
  • there were 211 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 24.4%)
