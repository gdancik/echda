EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (07/13/2020)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 07/09/2020,

  • there were 715 new daily reported confirmed/probable cases (an increase of 8.5%)
  • there were 24 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 35.1%)
  • there were 598.6 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 21%)
