EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (06/22/2020)

In New Haven county, for the week ending on 06/20/2020,

  • there were 151 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 26.7%)
  • there were 17 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 50%)
  • there were 378 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 32.6%)
