EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (08/24/2020)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 08/20/2020,

  • there were 813 new daily reported confirmed/probable cases (an increase of 26.2%)
  • there were 10 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 23.1%)
  • there were 54 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 6.9%)
