EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (07/06/2020)

In Hartford county, for the week ending on 07/02/2020,

  • there were 217 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (an increase of 41.8%)
  • there were 13 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 45.8%)
  • there were 197 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 16.2%)
