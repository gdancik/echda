EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (08/17/2020)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 08/13/2020,

  • there were 672 new daily reported confirmed/probable cases (a decrease of 45.9%)
  • there were 16 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 15.8%)
  • there were 56 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 5.1%)
