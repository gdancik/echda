EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (07/06/2020)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 07/02/2020,

  • there were 658 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (an increase of 31.1%)
  • there were 28 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 59.4%)
  • there were 715.2 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 24.9%)
