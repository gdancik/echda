EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (06/29/2020)

In Hartford county, for the week ending on 06/27/2020,

  • there were 182 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 2.7%)
  • there were 19 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 17.4%)
  • there were 207 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 29.4%)
