EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (06/29/2020)

In New Haven county, for the week ending on 06/27/2020,

  • there were 114 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 24.5%)
  • there were 12 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 29.4%)
  • there were 264 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 30.2%)
