EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (06/13/2020)

In Fairfield county, for the week ending on 06/12/2020,

  • there were 257 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 50.4%)
  • there were 36 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 16.3%)
  • there were 679 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 28.7%)
