EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly County Update (06/01/2020)

In New Haven county, for the week ending on 05/30/2020,

  • there were 418 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 44%)
  • there were 69 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 19.8%)
  • there were 1553 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 25.2%)
