EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (05/29/2020)

In Hartford county, for the week ending on 05/27/2020,

  • there were 779 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 42.9%)
  • there were 75 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 44%)
  • there were 1189 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 26.7%)
