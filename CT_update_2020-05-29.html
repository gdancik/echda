EIDA/COVID in CT Weekly Update (05/29/2020)

In the state of CT, for the week ending on 05/27/2020,

  • there were 2351 new daily reported lab-confirmed cases (a decrease of 37.2%)
  • there were 244 new daily reported COVID-19 associated deaths (a decrease of 32.8%)
  • there were 4897 current hospitalizations (a decrease of 24.7%)
